Help & shortcuts

Canvas editing (needs ✋ Edit on — nothing is drawn on top, you grab the real thing)
• Grab the center (click on it) and drag to move it.
• Grab a limb (click on it) and drag to aim it.
• Shift-click adds another center (forests / rhizome networks); ✕ multi clears extras.
• Turn ✋ Edit off to re-center and place limbs purely by the seed.

Shortcuts
R randomize params · M mutate (nudge) · S random seed · C reset center · G gallery · Ctrl+Z undo · Ctrl+Shift+Z redo · Esc close

Ideas
• Lock 🔒 the params you like, then Randomize / Mutate to explore the rest.
• Styles: Root system, Dense tree, Rhizome, Windswept, Neuron.
• Breeze animates the wind live; Wind ↓ = gravity for hanging roots.
• Gallery shows 12 seeds — click one to load it.
• Export a huge PNG for print, or a WebM seed-sweep / growth video.
